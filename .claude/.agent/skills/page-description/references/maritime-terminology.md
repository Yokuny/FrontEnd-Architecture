# Maritime Industry Terminology Reference

## Vessel Types & Classifications

### Commercial Vessels
- **Bulk Carrier**: Transports dry bulk cargo (grain, coal, iron ore)
- **Container Ship**: Carries standardized containers (TEU = Twenty-foot Equivalent Unit)
- **Tanker**: Oil tanker, chemical tanker, LNG/LPG carrier
- **General Cargo**: Multipurpose vessels, breakbulk cargo
- **Ro-Ro**: Roll-on/Roll-off vessels for vehicles
- **Reefer**: Refrigerated cargo vessels

### Offshore Support Vessels (OSV)
- **PSV (Platform Supply Vessel)**: Supplies offshore platforms with cargo, fuel, water
- **AHTS (Anchor Handling Tug Supply)**: Towing and anchor handling for rigs
- **MPSV (Multi-Purpose Support Vessel)**: Versatile offshore operations
- **ROV Support**: Remotely Operated Vehicle operations
- **FSV (Fast Supply Vessel)**: Rapid crew transfer and light cargo

### Specialized Vessels
- **Drillship**: Mobile offshore drilling units
- **Jack-up Rig**: Self-elevating drilling platform
- **Semi-submersible**: Floating drilling platform
- **Survey Vessel**: Geophysical and hydrographic surveys
- **Cable Layer**: Submarine cable installation
- **Dredger**: Harbor deepening and land reclamation

## Vessel Specifications & Measurements

### Tonnage Measurements
- **DWT (Deadweight Tonnage)**: Maximum weight a vessel can carry (cargo + fuel + stores + crew)
- **GRT (Gross Registered Tonnage)**: Total internal volume
- **NRT (Net Registered Tonnage)**: Cargo-carrying volume
- **Displacement**: Total weight of vessel and contents
- **Light Ship Weight**: Vessel weight empty

### Dimensions
- **LOA (Length Overall)**: Total length of vessel
- **LBP (Length Between Perpendiculars)**: Design waterline length
- **Beam**: Maximum width
- **Draft**: Depth of vessel below waterline
- **Freeboard**: Distance from waterline to main deck
- **Air Draft**: Height above waterline (for bridges/cranes)

## Charter Types & Commercial Terms

### Charter Party Types
- **Voyage Charter**: Hire for single voyage, owner pays operating costs
- **Time Charter**: Hire for time period, charterer pays fuel/port costs
- **Bareboat Charter (Demise Charter)**: Charterer takes full control and responsibility
- **Trip Charter**: Time charter for specific route only
- **COA (Contract of Affreightment)**: Series of voyages over time

### Financial Terms
- **Freight Rate**: Payment for cargo transport ($/ton or lump sum)
- **Hire Rate**: Daily/monthly charter payment ($/day)
- **TCE (Time Charter Equivalent)**: Standardized earning metric
- **Worldscale**: Tanker freight rate index system
- **Laytime**: Allowed time for loading/unloading
- **Demurrage**: Penalty for exceeding laytime
- **Despatch**: Bonus for completing early
- **Off-hire**: Period when vessel unavailable to charterer

### Charter Clauses
- **Speed & Consumption Warranty**: Guaranteed performance
- **Weather Working Days**: Laytime calculation method
- **Safe Port Clause**: Port must be safe for vessel
- **War Risk Clause**: Avoidance of conflict zones
- **Ice Clause**: Vessel not required to enter icebound ports
- **Force Majeure**: Exemption for uncontrollable events

## Operations & Navigation

### Operational Modes
- **Steaming/Passage**: Vessel underway at sea
- **Maneuvering**: Harbor entry/exit, berthing
- **At Anchor**: Stationary, engine idling
- **DP (Dynamic Positioning)**: Station-keeping using thrusters and GPS
- **Stand-by**: Waiting for instructions, ready state
- **Drifting**: No power, moving with currents

### Port Operations
- **Alongside**: Moored at berth
- **Shifting**: Moving within port
- **NOR (Notice of Readiness)**: Vessel ready to load/discharge
- **Pilot**: Local navigator for harbor transit
- **Tug Assistance**: Harbor maneuvering support
- **Line Handling**: Mooring operations
- **Stevedoring**: Cargo handling services
- **Port Clearance**: Customs and immigration

### Navigation Terms
- **ETA (Estimated Time of Arrival)**
- **ETD (Estimated Time of Departure)**
- **CPA (Closest Point of Approach)**
- **Course**: Direction of travel (degrees)
- **Speed**: Knots (nautical miles per hour)
- **Heading**: Direction vessel is pointing
- **Track**: Actual path traveled
- **Drift**: Current-induced movement

## Maintenance & Technical

### Maintenance Types
- **Preventive Maintenance (PM)**: Scheduled routine maintenance
- **Corrective Maintenance (CM)**: Repairs after breakdown
- **Predictive Maintenance (PdM)**: Condition-based maintenance
- **Running Maintenance**: During operational periods
- **Dry-Docking**: Out-of-water hull and propulsion work

### Equipment Systems
- **Main Engine (ME)**: Propulsion engine
- **Auxiliary Engine (AE)**: Generator sets
- **Boiler**: Steam generation
- **Purifier**: Fuel/oil cleaning
- **Compressor**: Compressed air systems
- **HVAC**: Heating, ventilation, air conditioning
- **Ballast System**: Stability control
- **Bilge System**: Water removal
- **Steering Gear**: Rudder control

### Maintenance Metrics
- **MTBF (Mean Time Between Failures)**: Reliability metric
- **MTTR (Mean Time To Repair)**: Repair speed metric
- **Availability**: Uptime percentage
- **OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)**
- **Failure Rate**: Breakdowns per unit time
- **PM Compliance**: % of scheduled maintenance completed

## Fuel & Consumption

### Fuel Types
- **HFO (Heavy Fuel Oil)**: IFO 180, IFO 380
- **MDO (Marine Diesel Oil)**: Distillate fuel
- **MGO (Marine Gas Oil)**: Low sulfur diesel
- **VLSFO (Very Low Sulfur Fuel Oil)**: < 0.5% sulfur
- **ULSFO (Ultra Low Sulfur Fuel Oil)**: < 0.1% sulfur
- **LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas)**: Alternative fuel
- **Methanol**: Alternative fuel

### Consumption Metrics
- **MT/day (Metric Tons per day)**: Daily consumption rate
- **SFOC (Specific Fuel Oil Consumption)**: g/kWh efficiency
- **ROB (Remaining On Board)**: Current fuel quantity
- **Bunker**: Fuel supply/refueling
- **Bunker Delivery Note (BDN)**: Fuel receipt certificate
- **Fuel Quality Certificate**: ISO 8217 compliance

## Environmental Compliance

### IMO Regulations
- **MARPOL**: Marine pollution prevention
  - Annex I: Oil pollution
  - Annex II: Noxious liquid substances
  - Annex III: Packaged harmful substances
  - Annex IV: Sewage
  - Annex V: Garbage
  - Annex VI: Air pollution

- **CII (Carbon Intensity Indicator)**: Annual rating (A-E scale)
- **EEXI (Energy Efficiency Existing Ship Index)**: Design efficiency
- **DCS (Data Collection System)**: Fuel consumption reporting
- **MRV (Monitoring, Reporting, Verification)**: EU regulation
- **EU ETS (Emissions Trading System)**: Carbon allowances
- **IMO 2020**: 0.5% sulfur cap globally
- **ECA (Emission Control Area)**: 0.1% sulfur zones

### Ballast & Waste
- **BWM (Ballast Water Management)**: Treatment systems
- **BWMS (Ballast Water Management System)**: IMO D-2 standard
- **Garbage Management Plan**: MARPOL Annex V
- **Oil Record Book (ORB)**: Discharge tracking
- **Garbage Record Book**: Disposal log

## Safety & Compliance

### International Conventions
- **SOLAS (Safety of Life at Sea)**: Ship safety standards
- **ISM Code (International Safety Management)**: Safety management system
- **ISPS Code (International Ship and Port Facility Security)**: Security measures
- **STCW (Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping)**: Crew qualifications
- **MLC (Maritime Labour Convention)**: Seafarer welfare

### Certificates & Documents
- **Class Certificate**: Classification society approval
- **Safety Management Certificate (SMC)**: ISM compliance
- **Document of Compliance (DOC)**: Company ISM cert
- **IOPP Certificate**: Oil pollution prevention
- **IAPP Certificate**: Air pollution prevention
- **Cargo Ship Safety Certificate**: SOLAS compliance
- **Minimum Safe Manning Document**: Crew requirements
- **Tonnage Certificate**: Official measurements

### Inspections & Audits
- **PSC (Port State Control)**: Flag state inspections
- **SIRE (Ship Inspection Report Programme)**: Oil major vetting
- **CDI (Chemical Distribution Institute)**: Chemical tanker vetting
- **OVMSA (Offshore Vessel Management Self-Assessment)**: Offshore vetting
- **Class Survey**: Annual, intermediate, special surveys
- **Internal Audit**: SMS compliance checks
- **Flag State Inspection**: Registry compliance

### Safety Equipment
- **LSA (Life Saving Appliances)**: Lifeboats, rafts, jackets
- **FFA (Fire Fighting Appliances)**: Hoses, extinguishers, systems
- **PPE (Personal Protective Equipment)**: Safety gear
- **GMDSS (Global Maritime Distress and Safety System)**: Emergency communications
- **EPIRB (Emergency Position Indicating Radio Beacon)**: Distress beacon
- **SART (Search and Rescue Transponder)**: Location transponder

## Crew & Manning

### Ranks & Positions
**Deck Department:**
- Master (Captain)
- Chief Officer (Chief Mate)
- Second Officer
- Third Officer
- Bosun (Boatswain)
- Able Seaman (AB)
- Ordinary Seaman (OS)

**Engine Department:**
- Chief Engineer
- Second Engineer
- Third Engineer
- Fourth Engineer
- Electrician
- Fitter
- Oiler/Wiper

**Catering:**
- Chief Cook
- Steward

**Offshore Specific:**
- OIM (Offshore Installation Manager)
- Crane Operator
- Deck Foreman
- Roustabout

### Certifications (STCW)
- **COC (Certificate of Competency)**: Officer licenses
- **COP (Certificate of Proficiency)**: Specialized training
- **Deck Watch Rating**: Bridge watchkeeping
- **Engine Watch Rating**: Engine room watchkeeping
- **Basic Safety Training**: Mandatory for all seafarers
- **Advanced Fire Fighting**
- **Medical First Aid**
- **GMDSS Radio Operator**

### Work Schedules
- **Rotation**: Work/rest cycle (e.g., 4 weeks on / 4 weeks off)
- **Watch System**: 4-on-8-off, 6-on-6-off
- **Rest Hours**: Minimum 10 hours in 24-hour period
- **Sign-on/Sign-off**: Contract start/end
- **Repatriation**: Return to home country

## Offshore Oil & Gas Specific

### Operations
- **Mobilization (Mob)**: Preparation and transit to field
- **Demobilization (Demob)**: Completion and return from field
- **DP Operations**: Dynamic positioning work
- **Anchor Handling**: AHTS positioning rigs
- **ROV Operations**: Subsea work support
- **Standby Duty**: Emergency response vessel

### Contracts & Rates
- **Day Rate**: Daily charter rate
- **Utilization Rate**: % of time productively employed
- **Standby Rate**: Reduced rate when waiting
- **Spot Market**: Short-term charter opportunities
- **Term Contract**: Long-term charter agreement
- **Call-off Contract**: Framework agreement

### Equipment & Capabilities
- **DP Class**: Dynamic positioning capability (DP1, DP2, DP3)
- **Deck Cargo Capacity**: Tonnes, square meters
- **Liquid Mud Capacity**: Cubic meters
- **Fuel Capacity**: Cubic meters
- **Accommodation**: Number of berths
- **Crane Capacity**: SWL (Safe Working Load) in tonnes
- **Clear Deck Area**: Square meters
